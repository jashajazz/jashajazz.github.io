Power BI

Basic Sales Dashboard

Basic sales dashboard with some visualizations. Original data was transformed and cleaned, connented to Power BI, modeled, added with measures, visualized.

Source: data is from openly shared reasearch data in UCI Repo (https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Online%20Retail).

Market view

Quick view on the markets based on the registered business field. Ability to search market, see how the market is distributed, view essential figures based on the company revenues in the market. Can be extended with detailed info. Actual company IDs are not used.

Source: data is from openly data APIs https://www.vero.fi/tietoa-verohallinnosta/tilastot/avoin_dat/ and https://avoindata.prh.fi/ytj.html

Government Purchasing

Finnish governement purchasing data exploration. Data available openly (avoindata.fi) and includes governmental purchases ministries and purchase suppliers.
