Time-Series with stock prices¶

Visualizing stock prices of one Finnish company which stock are quoted in Helsinki Stock Exchange. Idea is to visualize time-serie data in understandable form, create few known indicators, and present the result.

  • Daily end price
  • Daily exchange volumes
  • Bollinger bands
  • Volatility

Importing relevant libraries¶

  • Numpy for numerical Python operations.
  • Pandas for dataframes.
  • Seaborn to visualize the data.

Creating more indicators from data¶

We'll create several new indicators as follows:

  • 20-day moving average
  • standard deviation
  • bollinger bands
In [104]:
df["20_moving"] = df["Close"].rolling(window=20).mean()
std_dev = df["Close"].rolling(window=20).std()
df["bol_lo"] = df["Close"].rolling(window=20).mean() - (2*std_dev)
df["bol_hi"] = df["Close"].rolling(window=20).mean() + (2*std_dev)
